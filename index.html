ScorpionCrypto — Whitepaper

Technical overview, tokenomics, governance and roadmap.

1. Executive Summary

ScorpionCrypto is a decentralized token project focused on community, security and utility. This whitepaper summarizes the protocol architecture and token distribution strategy.

2. Tokenomics

Total supply: 1,000,000,000 SCORP. Distribution: Community & Staking 40%, Team 25% (vested), Liquidity 20%, Reserve 15%.

3. Governance & Vesting

Governance will be launched after key milestones; team tokens are vested to align long-term incentives. Full schedules will be published on the site and GitHub.

4. Security & Audits

Smart contracts will be audited by third-party auditors before listings. Security-first approach to protect liquidity and user funds.

5. Roadmap

Short-term: launch & community. Mid-term: audits & listings. Long-term: staking, governance, ecosystem integrations.
